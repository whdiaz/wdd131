Site Name

Wanderlust Chronicles

This name was chosen because it reflects the spirit of adventure and the detailed accounts of travel experiences that the blog will offer. It captures the essence of exploring new places and sharing those experiences.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a personal travel blog where I share my travel experiences, photos, tips, and itineraries. The website will provide valuable content for travel enthusiasts looking for inspiration, advice, and detailed information on various travel destinations.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What are the top 10 must-visit attractions in Paris?

Scenario 2: How can I plan a week-long trip to Japan with a budget of $2000?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2a9d8f (Teal) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and buttons.

Secondary Color: #e9c46a (Yellow) - Used for background accents, highlights, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif - Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.).

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Used for all body text and paragraphs.
